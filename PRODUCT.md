# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Hackathon builders — Web3 devs, indie hackers, crypto-native teams — submitting to Monad hackathons with 24–72 hours until deadline. They're in crunch mode: stressed, sleep-deprived, seeking honest signal on whether their project will survive judging.

## Product Purpose

PreFlight simulates hackathon judging before submission. It runs 5 parallel validators (GitHub repo health, live app status, Monad contract verification, x402 payment flow, submission checklist), synthesizes an AI judge persona, and returns a readiness score, category breakdown, likely judge questions, and targeted improvement suggestions. Projects scoring ≥80% can mint a soulbound "PreFlight Ready" badge on Monad as proof of quality.

## Brand Personality

Blunt, hype-aware, credible. Like a senior dev who's also judged hackathons — tells you your app is broken with zero sugarcoating, then tells you exactly how to fix it in the time you have left.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS dashboards (Notion-soft, Tailwind card grids, cream/sand/white aesthetic)
- Overly corporate AI tools (soft gradients, friendly mascots, padded emptiness)
- Dev-tool aesthetic clones (dark VSCode lookalike, terminal-only)
- Anything that "softens the blow" — this tool needs to deliver uncomfortable truths clearly

## Design Principles

1. **Verdict first** — lead with the score, then the explanation. Builders need a number fast.
2. **Blunt > polished** — clarity over comfort. A 42% score should feel like a cold shower.
3. **Credibility through specificity** — exact reasoning for every flag. "Missing x402 replay guard" beats "payment issues."
4. **Celebrate the win** — when a builder hits ≥80%, make it feel earned. The badge mint is a moment.
5. **Speed is respect** — builders are in crunch mode; every interaction must be immediate and purposeful.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA. Reduced motion supported. Color is never the sole signal — score states use text labels alongside color. High-contrast black-on-yellow palette satisfies contrast requirements throughout.
